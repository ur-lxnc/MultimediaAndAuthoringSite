The Course

Multimedia and Authoring is a subject that bridges creative digital production with technical software proficiency. Throughout the course, students work with industry-standard tools to produce multimedia outputs that communicate ideas visually and interactively.

The two main projects — Rotoscoping and 3D Rendering — challenge students to learn both the artistic and technical dimensions of digital media: patience and precision in animation, and spatial thinking in three-dimensional environments.
